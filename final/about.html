About Me

I’m Laura, a passionate graphic designer with over 5 years of experience crafting unique visual identities and digital experiences. My journey began with a love for art and storytelling, which I’ve transformed into a career where I help brands connect with their audiences through compelling design.

I specialize in logo design, branding, UI/UX, and web development. Constant learning and adapting keep my work fresh and aligned with current trends. When I’m not designing, you can find me exploring nature or diving into new creative hobbies.
